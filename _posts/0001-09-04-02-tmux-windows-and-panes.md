Tmux:  Windows and Panes
------------------------

* To create a new tmux verticle split:  `Ctrl-b %`

* To create a new tmux horizontal split:  `Ctrl-b %`

* To move between pains, combine `Ctrl-b` with `Up`, `Down`, `Left` or `Right`

* To close a pain:  `Ctrl-b x`
